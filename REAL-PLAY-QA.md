# Real Play QA

Use this file to record real child games after V1.0 release.

## What To Record

- date
- child age or rough level, without personal identifying details
- difficulty mode
- child color
- move count
- completed or abandoned
- whether save/restore was used
- repeated missed captures
- repeated failure to rescue groups
- repeated meaningless moves
- unstable difficulty behavior
- crash, illegal move or touch-coordinate issue

## Reopening Engine Work

Do not reopen engine work for one unusual move. Reopen only for repeated serious issues:

- repeated missed captures
- repeated failure to rescue groups
- repeated meaningless moves
- repeated unstable difficulty behavior
- repeatable crash or illegal move

Date:
